About Me

My name is Alberto Rivera. I was born in Southern California and moved to Missouri when I was 17. It was rough, the only thing I wanted was to graduate with my high school friends. I attended the first semester of 12th grade in Missouri but once I turned 18 (legally an adult) I drove back to California to graduate with my class of 2004. It was a wonderful reunion and graduation, I was glad I got to drive back but sad at the same time. I didn't mean it as a rebellious act against my guardian at the time, but it was the one thing I really ever wanted during High School. After graduation I worked at McDonalds and saved enough money to drive back to Missouri. It was there I completed my recruiting journey to the United States Air Force

I always wanted to be a Police Detective growing up. This is why when I joined the Air Force I chose the Security Forces career field. From my research before joining the military, it was this career field where I read I could develop myself on learning the Law Enforcement basics, hopefully later lateral training into becoming an investigator. Was I wrong... my first assignment took me to an area in Las Vegas where I protected aircraft on a flight line. After 2 years it wasn't what I expected. Long story short, some initial assignments or first-time experiences isn't what you always picture it or imagine it being. After 15 years in the Air Force, I do not regret a single thing!
